Hi, I’m Wesley!

High end, natural light, fashion portrait photographer.

“For me a camera is like a sketchbook. An instrument of intuition and spontaneity”

My names Wesley and I love photography…

 

For me photography has always been a source of great pleasure. From storytelling street photography to spectacular landscape and wildlife photography, I love it all. There’s nothing more satisfying than a razor sharp close up or a creamy wide open Bokeh shot. My true passion, however has come from photographing people, this is where I feel most comfortable.

A photoshoot in my eyes is one of the most vulnerable of experiences you can put yourself in and trusting someone to capture you in the best version of yourself is crucial.

I pride myself on being able to connect with clients from all backgrounds and build a rapport to help produce stunning visuals for portfolios, magazines, album covers and online content.

If I could tagline my photography in a simple phrase it would be…..‘Embracing natural Light’ I love the feelings involved with natural light photography and playing around with the light that is available, to create authentic and natural portraits.

I’m inspired by many wonderful artists such as Marco De Sanris, Rico Reinhold, Jessica Kobeissi, Diana Kenyeres and Mannie Ortiz and I would like to say that my work is heavily influenced by these tremendous photographers.
